Ask Us Anything 💬 WhatsApp Us
How much does website development cost in Patna? ▾
A basic business website starts at ₹8,000–₹15,000. An advanced website with custom features, animations and CMS is ₹20,000–₹60,000. E-commerce stores start at ₹20,000. All quotes are transparent — no hidden charges.
How long does it take to build a website? ▾
A standard business website takes 7–14 days. Complex e-commerce or multi-page platforms take 3–6 weeks. We always provide a clear timeline before starting and communicate any changes immediately.
Do you provide SEO services along with web development? ▾
Yes. Every website we build includes foundational on-page SEO — optimised headings, meta tags, schema markup and a sitemap. We also offer monthly SEO retainers to continuously improve your rankings in Patna and Bihar.
Do you develop apps for both Android and iOS? ▾
Yes. We build cross-platform apps using React Native and Flutter (one codebase for both platforms) and native apps in Swift (iOS) or Kotlin (Android) when maximum performance is required. We handle App Store and Google Play submission.
What e-commerce platforms do you build on? ▾
We primarily build on WooCommerce and Shopify for most businesses, and on fully custom stacks (Next.js + Node.js) for complex marketplaces or unique requirements. We integrate Razorpay, Paytm, PayU and Stripe payment gateways.
Can you help my business rank on Google in Bihar? ▾
Yes — local SEO for Bihar is our strength. We optimise your Google Business Profile, build local citations, target location-based keywords and create content that signals local relevance to Google. Most clients see ranking improvements within 60–90 days.
What is AI automation and how can it help my business? ▾
AI automation replaces repetitive tasks with intelligent software. Examples: a chatbot that answers customer queries 24/7, an AI that reads and files incoming documents, or a system that scores leads and adds them to your CRM automatically. We custom-build these for your specific workflow.
What is IoT and do I need it for my business? ▾
IoT connects physical devices (sensors, cameras, machines) to the internet so you can monitor and control them remotely. Examples: a restaurant using IoT to monitor refrigerator temperatures, a retail store tracking footfall in real time, or a factory monitoring equipment health. If you want to digitise physical operations, IoT is for you.
Do you offer website maintenance after launch? ▾
Yes. All our maintenance plans cover security updates, plugin/framework updates, content changes, uptime monitoring, daily backups and priority support. Plans start at ₹2,500/month and are tailored to your needs.
How do I get started with Axivon Technologies? ▾
Simply fill out our contact form, WhatsApp us or call directly. We'll schedule a free 30-minute discovery call, understand your requirements and send you a detailed proposal with timeline and pricing — all within 24 hours. No commitment required.
Do you work with businesses outside Patna and Bihar? ▾
Absolutely. While we're based in Patna, we work with clients across India — Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad and internationally. Our remote collaboration tools and processes are solid. Geography is never a barrier.
What digital marketing services do you offer? ▾
We run Google Search Ads, Google Display Ads, Meta (Facebook & Instagram) Ads, YouTube Ads, WhatsApp marketing campaigns and email marketing sequences. All campaigns include conversion tracking, A/B testing and weekly performance reports.
Do you provide cloud hosting and server management? ▾
Yes. We set up and manage cloud infrastructure on AWS, Google Cloud and Azure. This includes auto-scaling, 99.9% uptime monitoring, security hardening, SSL management and cost optimisation. Many clients reduce their cloud bill by 30–50% after switching to our managed setup.
Can you redesign my existing website? ▾
Yes — website redesign is one of our most common projects. We audit your existing site's performance, SEO and UX issues, then redesign it from the ground up while preserving your existing content and search rankings. Redesign projects start at ₹10,000.
Will my website be visible in Google search after launch? ▾
Every website we build is submitted to Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools on launch day. We ensure your sitemap, robots.txt, canonical URLs and schema markup are correctly configured. Basic indexing typically happens within 7–14 days of launch.
