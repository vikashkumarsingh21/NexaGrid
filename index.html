Ask Us Anything
How much does a website cost in Patna? ▾
Website pricing depends on complexity. A professional business website starts at ₹8,000–₹25,000. E-commerce stores range from ₹20,000 to ₹1,00,000+. We offer transparent, itemized quotes — no hidden charges. Contact us for a free estimate tailored to your needs.
How long does it take to build a website? ▾
A standard business website takes 7–14 days. E-commerce and custom web applications typically take 3–6 weeks depending on features. We provide a clear timeline at the start of every project.
Do you provide SEO services along with web development? ▾
Yes! Every website we build includes basic on-page SEO — optimized headings, meta tags, schema markup, sitemap and fast loading. We also offer monthly SEO retainers to help you rank for keywords like "best [your business] in Patna" and drive consistent organic traffic.
Do you develop mobile apps for Android and iOS? ▾
Absolutely. We build native Android and iOS apps as well as cross-platform apps using React Native and Flutter. Cross-platform apps are more cost-effective and work great for most business use cases, giving you both platforms for the price of one.
Will my website work on mobile phones? ▾
Every website we build is 100% mobile-responsive and tested on real devices. With over 80% of Indian internet users on mobile, we treat mobile-first design as a non-negotiable standard, not an optional add-on.
Can you help my business rank on Google in Patna? ▾
Yes. Our SEO team specializes in local SEO for Patna and Bihar — including Google Business Profile optimization, local keyword targeting, citation building and content marketing. Most clients see measurable ranking improvements within 60–90 days.
Do you offer post-launch maintenance and support? ▾
Yes. We offer flexible maintenance plans covering security updates, content changes, performance monitoring, backups and 24/7 uptime alerts. You'll always have a dedicated point of contact who knows your project inside-out.
What makes Axivon Technologies different from other agencies in Bihar? ▾
Three things: quality, transparency and local expertise. We build to global standards while understanding Bihar's market deeply. We don't use templates where custom is better. And we communicate clearly — no technical jargon, no vanishing after delivery. We're your long-term digital partner.
