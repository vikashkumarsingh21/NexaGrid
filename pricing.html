Pricing Questions Answered

Everything you need to know before making a decision.

A professional landing page starts at ₹8,000. A complete business website with CMS and SEO starts at ₹25,000. E-commerce stores start at ₹35,000. All pricing is transparent with zero hidden charges. You'll receive a detailed quote before any work begins.
Never. Our pricing philosophy is simple: what we quote is what you pay. Domain registration, hosting fees, third-party plugins and any other costs are discussed and agreed upon before the project starts — they're never added to invoices later.
A cross-platform app (Android + iOS) starts at ₹40,000 using React Native or Flutter. Native Android starts at ₹40,000 and native iOS starts at ₹50,000. Enterprise-grade apps with complex backends, real-time features or AI integration are priced based on scope — typically ₹1,00,000 to ₹5,00,000+.
Our Local SEO plan (₹6,000/mo) includes Google Business Profile optimization, local citations, map ranking and 10 target keywords. Business SEO (₹12,000/mo) adds full on-page optimization, content creation and 20+ keywords. Enterprise SEO (₹25,000/mo) includes technical SEO, link building, PR outreach and national/international targeting. All plans include monthly reporting.
A landing page or basic website takes 7–10 business days. A full business website with CMS takes 14–21 days. E-commerce stores typically take 21–30 days. Enterprise projects run 30–60 days depending on scope. We provide a detailed timeline in your project proposal before work begins.
For standard projects we require 50% upfront and 50% on final delivery and approval. For larger projects (above ₹50,000) we offer milestone-based payments tied to project phases. We accept UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfer (NEFT/IMPS), and cash for local Patna clients.
Yes, for projects above ₹50,000 we can structure flexible monthly payment schedules. Contact us to discuss your budget and we'll create a plan that works for your cash flow — usually 3 to 6 monthly instalments.
All plans include post-launch support: Starter gets 1 month, Business gets 3 months and Enterprise gets 6 months. After that, you can opt into our maintenance plans starting at ₹2,000/month covering updates, security patches, backups and uptime monitoring.
Our maintenance plans cover monthly security updates, plugin/dependency updates, daily automated backups, uptime monitoring with alerts, content changes (up to 5 per month), and a monthly performance report. We have three tiers starting from ₹2,000/month.
Most clients in Patna and Bihar see measurable improvements within 60–90 days for local keywords. Competitive national keywords typically take 4–6 months. SEO is a long-term investment — clients on ongoing plans typically see 3–5x organic traffic growth within 12 months.
Hosting setup and configuration is included in all plans. The annual hosting cost (typically ₹3,000 – ₹8,000/year depending on requirements) is paid directly to the hosting provider in your name so you own it completely. We recommend and set up the best hosting for your specific needs.
Yes. Our cross-platform development using React Native or Flutter gives you both Android and iOS apps from a single codebase. This typically saves 30–40% compared to building two native apps separately. For most business applications, cross-platform performance is indistinguishable from native.
IoT pricing covers hardware integration consulting, firmware/embedded software, cloud backend, real-time dashboard and mobile app access. Basic IoT projects (₹30,000–₹60,000) handle simple sensor monitoring. Industrial IoT covers factory automation and predictive maintenance. Hardware costs (sensors, controllers, microcontrollers) are separate and sourced to your specifications.
