Our Journey

From a Small Idea to
Bihar's Leading Digital Agency

Every milestone in our story has been shaped by one goal — helping businesses succeed in the digital world.

🌱
2020 — Beginning

The Seed Is Planted

NexaGrid was founded in Patna by a team of passionate developers and designers who believed Bihar's businesses deserved world-class digital products. We started with a laptop, a shared vision and an obsession with quality.

⚡
2021 — Early Projects

First Wins, Huge Lessons

We delivered our first 20 projects — websites and apps for local restaurants, education institutes and retail shops in Patna. Each project taught us something new and deepened our understanding of Bihar's unique business landscape.

📈
2022 — Growth

Scaling Up, Branching Out

Word spread fast. We expanded our team, added mobile app development, SEO and AI automation to our service portfolio. Clients from across Bihar — and beyond — started partnering with us for their digital transformation journeys.

🏆
2023–24 — Current Stage

100+ Projects, 50+ Happy Clients

Today, NexaGrid is recognised as Bihar's premium digital agency. We have delivered 100+ projects, maintain a 98% client satisfaction rate and operate a 24/7 support system. Our work spans healthcare, education, hospitality, retail and manufacturing.

🚀
2025 & Beyond — Future Vision

Building Bihar's Digital Future

We're expanding our AI and cloud capabilities, building proprietary SaaS products and setting up partnerships across India. Our vision is to make NexaGrid the go-to digital partner for every ambitious business in India — starting from Patna, reaching the world.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you want to know about NexaGrid — answered honestly.

What is NexaGrid and what do you do? ▾
NexaGrid is a premium digital agency and software development company based in Patna, Bihar. We specialise in website development, mobile app development, SEO, AI automation, cloud solutions and IoT development. We serve startups, SMEs and enterprises across Bihar and India — helping them build digital products that drive real business growth.
Where is NexaGrid located and do you work with clients outside Patna? ▾
NexaGrid is headquartered in Patna, Bihar, India. While we have deep roots in Bihar, we work with clients from across India and internationally. Our remote-first workflow means geography is never a limitation — you get the same quality of service whether you're in Patna, Mumbai, Delhi or abroad.
How many projects has NexaGrid completed? ▾
We have successfully delivered 100+ digital projects for 50+ businesses across various industries. Our portfolio includes websites, e-commerce stores, mobile apps, IoT dashboards, SaaS platforms and AI automation tools — with a 98% client satisfaction rate across all projects.
What industries does NexaGrid serve? ▾
We serve businesses across healthcare, education, restaurants, hotels and hospitality, retail and e-commerce, startups, manufacturing and more. We bring domain-specific knowledge to every engagement — understanding your industry's users, compliance needs and market dynamics, not just the technology.
Does NexaGrid work with early-stage startups? ▾
Absolutely — we love working with startups. We have dedicated startup-friendly packages including lean MVP development, rapid prototyping and growth-stage scaling. We understand startup constraints and help founders build the right product, fast, without wasting runway on unnecessary features.
What technologies does NexaGrid use? ▾
We work with a modern, carefully chosen technology stack: React, Next.js, Node.js, Python for web and backend; Flutter and React Native for mobile apps; AWS and Google Cloud for infrastructure; MongoDB and PostgreSQL for databases; and various AI/ML frameworks for automation. We select the best stack for each project's specific needs — not a one-size-fits-all approach.
How long does a typical project take to complete? ▾
Timelines depend on project scope and complexity. A standard business website takes 7–14 days. A custom web application or e-commerce store takes 30–60 days. Mobile apps typically take 45–90 days. AI automation projects vary from 2–8 weeks. We always share a detailed timeline before starting and keep you updated at every milestone.
Does NexaGrid provide post-launch maintenance and support? ▾
Yes, absolutely. We offer 24/7 support and flexible maintenance plans after every project launch. Your named point of contact — someone who knows your project inside-out — is always available for updates, security patches, performance monitoring, bug fixes and new feature additions. We're a long-term partner, not a one-off vendor.
Can NexaGrid handle both design and development for my project? ▾
Yes — NexaGrid is a full-service agency. We cover UI/UX design, branding and visual identity, front-end development, back-end engineering, cloud deployment, SEO and ongoing support — all under one roof. You don't need to coordinate multiple vendors; we handle the complete journey from concept to live product.
How do I get started with NexaGrid? ▾
Getting started is easy. Fill out our contact form at nexagrid.in/contact, WhatsApp us directly, or book a free consultation call. We'll understand your requirements in a 30-minute discovery call, then send you a clear proposal, project estimate and timeline within 24 hours — no commitments, no jargon, no pressure.
