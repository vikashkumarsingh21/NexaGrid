MediCare Plus

How we turned a struggling clinic into Bihar's most-booked healthcare platform

Healthcare · Mobile App · Web Portal

Client Overview

MediCare Plus is a multi-specialty clinic chain based in Patna with 4 locations. Before partnering with NexaGrid, they relied entirely on phone bookings, lost over 60% of after-hours inquiries, and had no digital presence.

The Challenge

  • No online appointment booking system
  • Patients calling after hours with no way to book
  • Zero organic search visibility in Patna
  • High no-show rates (38%) with no reminders
  • Manual patient records causing billing errors

Our Solution

  • Custom patient portal with 24/7 booking
  • Android & iOS app with WhatsApp reminders
  • Doctor availability calendar with real-time slots
  • Integrated billing and medical records module
  • Local SEO campaign targeting Patna keywords

Results at a Glance

12,000+ Active Users
60% Faster Booking
4.8★ App Rating
3X Engagement Growth

Technologies Used

React Native Node.js MongoDB Firebase Razorpay WhatsApp API Google Maps

Implementation Timeline

Week 1–2 Discovery & Research

Stakeholder interviews, patient journey mapping, competitor analysis

Week 3–4 UI/UX Design

Wireframes, prototypes, user testing with clinic staff and patients

Week 5–10 Development

Full-stack portal, React Native app, WhatsApp integration, payment gateway

Week 11–12 Testing & Launch

QA across 8 devices, staff training, phased rollout across 4 clinics
